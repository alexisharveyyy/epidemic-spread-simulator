Vaccination Scenarios

How does pre-existing immunity from vaccination change outbreak dynamics?

HOW THIS WORKS

This page is a simplified model. Full vaccination dynamics would require an SEIRV compartmental model with vaccination rates and waning immunity, but starting some fraction of the population in the recovered (immune) compartment is a reasonable approximation for steady-state vaccination coverage at outbreak start. Two SIR runs are issued, one with no immunity and one with the chosen coverage; the results are compared side by side.
