SCIENTIFIC FOUNDATIONS

The compartmental model implemented here is the SIR model from Kermack & McKendrick (1927), A Contribution to the Mathematical Theory of Epidemics. Subsequent compartmental modeling literature — Anderson & May (1991), Hethcote (2000), Delamater et al. (2019) — informed the parameter choices and language used throughout the application.
